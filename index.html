The Artist

Storyteller. Spirit. Multipotentialite.

Born Bongani Glen Sibanyoni in Mabopane, Pretoria, Bongani Givethanks is a self-taught pianist whose talent discovered itself before any institution could name it. Fuelled by an irrepressible creative drive, he expanded from the piano into sound design, music production, composition, and vocal artistry — becoming one of South African music's most distinctly-voiced independent voices.

He describes his work as African Creative Music — a living fusion of South African jazz, mbaqanga, traditional song forms, and electronic music. His is not genre tourism. It is a philosophy: that music is a spiritual medium, a vehicle for storytelling, and an instrument for social change.

His output spans addressing gender-based violence in the visceral EP Indoda; reclaiming Ndebele ancestral heritage in the documentary-linked single A Sonic Remembrance: Chief Mngobane ka Gegana; and crafting feel-good Nujazz through his Nujazz Shandis series — each project a different register of the same abiding purpose.

The Company

Mvumo Productions

Founded by Bongani Givethanks, Mvumo Productions is a Pretoria-based music production company with a mission that goes beyond commercial output. It exists to develop and stimulate artistic creativity and professionalism through producing, commissioning, and presenting productions.

At its heart, Mvumo Productions is a system — an intentional creative infrastructure for sustaining live music ecosystems, supporting emerging artists, and building community through music.
